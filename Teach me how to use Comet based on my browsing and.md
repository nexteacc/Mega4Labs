# Teach me how to use Comet based on my browsing and query history, highlight the most recent feature updates, suggest five personalized and specific queries I should try, and surface the most compelling use case and prompt examples shared on social media.

Here’s how to get started and leverage Comet for maximal productivity, tailored to your browsing history and interests. This guide covers key workflows, highlights the latest feature updates, offers personalized query inspiration, and surfaces the most compelling use cases and prompt examples:

***

**How to Use Comet: Quick Start**

- **Install \& Setup:** Download Comet for Windows or Mac from the official site. Follow the installation instructions; Comet will auto-update to the latest version.
- **Search Smarter:** Perplexity AI powers the primary search, delivering verified, concise answers—ideal for deep research, quick fact-checking, or tracking diverse news sources.
- **Personal Search:** Comet can analyze your browsing history (with privacy controls) to deliver relevant results. Use natural language commands to find past pages and manage tabs efficiently.
- **Key Features:**
    - *Ask Button* for instant context/explanations on any open page.
    - *Summarize Button* to condense lengthy articles, news, and documentation.
    - *Email/Gmail Connector* for querying email and calendar directly from the browser.
    - *AdBlock,* bookmarks, and support for most Chrome extensions.
- **Security:** Built-in defenses against malicious content, with layered real-time protection. 1Password integration for effortless credential management and end-to-end encryption.

***

**Most Recent Feature Updates (2025):**

- **Background Assistants:** AI agents that proactively complete tasks in the background while you browse. (E.g., schedule meetings, draft replies in Gmail).[^1]
- **Email Assistant:** cc your AI on email threads to automate scheduling and reply drafts.[^1]
- **Shortcuts (“/”):** Instantly create and launch multi-step workflows or frequent queries with a slash—speed up repetitive tasks.[^2]
- **Mobile App Preview:** Comet now has a mobile preview for AI-powered, voice-first browsing.[^1]
- **Security Enhancements:** Advanced real-time prompt-injection protection and new collaboration with security researchers.[^3]
- **Integrated 1Password Extension:** Secure logins without friction, transparent credential use, and full privacy controls.[^4]
- **Enterprise Features:** Seamless Chrome extension support, SSO, policy controls, and intelligent automations for business.[^5]

**Compelling Use Cases and Prompt Examples Shared Online:**

- *Highlight text on any webpage to get instant explanation, counterpoints, or deeper context—no need to switch tabs or copy-paste*.[^6]
- *Automate multi-step research workflows (e.g., comparing business models, summarizing dozens of news sources, or building reading lists) by creating shortcuts with “/”*.[^2]
- *Transform an uploaded photo into an immersive video, then post it to social media using Comet Assistant* (example: real estate property showcase).[^7]
- *Enterprise productivity:* Vendor research, customer analysis, market intelligence, or content creation automated as multi-step AI workflows.[^5]

**Why Comet is Different (Most Compelling Use Case):**

Comet’s ability to combine deep AI search, personal browsing context, background assistants, and real-time workflow automation transforms everything from news research to business collaboration. Unlike old-school chatbots, Comet’s agents proactively handle tasks *for you* across browser, email, and mobile, freeing up your focus for what matters most.[^6][^3][^4][^5][^1]
