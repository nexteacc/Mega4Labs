# Comet × Perplexity Learning Hub

> A multilingual learning platform for mastering Comet Browser and Perplexity AI

## About

The Comet × Perplexity Learning Hub curates high-quality YouTube video tutorials to help users learn AI-powered research and productivity tools. We save users time by organizing scattered content from expert creators worldwide into a structured learning path—from beginner basics to advanced techniques. No brainrot. Updated weekly.

## Content

### Video Categories
- **Hero Featured**: Essential tutorials for getting started
- **Tutorial Library**: Step-by-step guides from basics to advanced workflows
- **Pro Reviews**: Expert analysis and browser comparisons (Comet vs Chrome, Arc, Atlas)
- **Quick Tips**: One-minute productivity hacks and hidden features

### Topics Covered
- Comet Browser setup and features
- Perplexity AI search techniques
- Browser automation and AI agents
- Research workflows and productivity
- Email management and form automation
- Travel planning and price comparison

### Languages
Available in English, Korean (한국어), Japanese (日本語), and Chinese (简体中文). Each language version includes localized interface and language-specific video content.

## Technical

Built with Next.js 16, TypeScript, and TailwindCSS. Features include:
- SSG/ISR rendering with 6-hour revalidation
- Schema.org structured data (VideoObject, ItemList, WebSite, Organization)
- Multilingual SEO with hreflang tags
- Mobile-responsive design
- PWA support
