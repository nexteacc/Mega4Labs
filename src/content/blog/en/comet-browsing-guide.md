Comet changes the way you browse, research, and work online. This quick guide shows how to start fast and use Comet's smart AI tools in daily life.

## Quick Start

### Install Comet
Download Comet for Windows or Mac from the official site. The browser updates automatically so you always get the newest version.

### Core Features

**AI Search**
Type any question. Comet gives clear, verified answers powered by Perplexity AI. Great for research, fact-checking, or reading the news.

**Personal Search**
Find any page you visited before, organize tabs, and search your own history using plain language.

**Helpful Tools**
- **Ask Button**: Get quick explanations on any page.
- **Summarize Button**: Turn long articles into short highlights.
- **Email Connector**: Ask about your Gmail or calendar directly.
- **AdBlock**: Blocks annoying ads.
- **Extensions**: Works with most Chrome extensions.

**Security First**
Comet blocks harmful websites in real time and connects with 1Password for safe logins.

## What's New in 2025

**Background Assistants**: Let AI help schedule meetings, reply to emails, or take notes while you browse.

**Shortcuts ("/")**: Run multi-step tasks with one simple slash command.

**Mobile Preview**: Use Comet on your phone with voice-based control.

**Advanced Security**: Protects you from prompt-injection and unsafe content.

**Enterprise Support**: Works with Chrome extensions, SSO, and business tools.

## Why Comet Feels Different

Comet mixes deep AI search with your personal context. It remembers what you read, automates research, and connects your apps. Unlike basic chatbots, Comet's assistants work quietly in the background so you can focus on results.

## Start Exploring

Highlight any text to get explanations, counterpoints, or summaries instantly. Use slash shortcuts to create workflows. The more you use Comet, the smarter it becomes.
